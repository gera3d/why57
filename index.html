Mutual Fit

Some projects move fast. Some should wait.

The best engagements start with a clear bottleneck, an owner on your side, and a first version that's worth shipping now.

Good fit

  • You can point to a real bottleneck costing time, revenue, or trust.
  • You want a sharp first release, not a giant 12-month rewrite.
  • Someone on your team can own feedback and decisions during the build.
  • You care about outcomes, not just buying dev hours.

Not yet

  • The problem is still vague and changes every time we describe it.
  • You want a bargain-bin build before validating the workflow.
  • No one can own the project internally once it goes live.
  • You need a generalist freelancer more than a scoped systems partner.

If this sounds like your situation, we can usually map the first version in one call.
