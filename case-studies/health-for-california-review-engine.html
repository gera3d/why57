The Problem

9,000 satisfied clients a year. Ten reviews to show for it.

Health for California is the largest independent health insurance marketplace in California — a platform that connects thousands of licensed agents with individuals and families shopping for coverage. These agents were good at their jobs. They built real relationships, helped real people find policies that fit their lives, and had genuinely happy clients.

But their digital reputation was almost nonexistent. As an organization, they had roughly 10 Google reviews total. Individual agents had none. When prospective clients searched for a local health insurance agent — the kind of search that happens thousands of times a day in California — Health for California's agents were invisible. Competitors with fewer clients and worse service were outranking them simply because they had reviews and HFC's agents didn't.

The real problem wasn't that clients were unhappy. It was that there was no system. No process that captured the moment of satisfaction and converted it into a public credential. Every happy customer who hung up the phone was a missed opportunity, and there were thousands of them.

What We Built

57Seconds — built specifically for HFC.

We built an end-to-end review acquisition engine — what became the foundation of our 57Seconds product. Every piece of the system was designed around one core insight: the best moment to ask for a review is immediately after the customer interaction, while the positive feeling is still fresh.

Agent-Specific Landing Pages

Every agent on the HFC platform got their own custom landing page — a digital profile showcasing their reviews, their credentials, and a direct link to leave a Google review. When a client clicked through from the SMS, they arrived at a page that felt personal, not generic.

Automated SMS Workflows

After each customer enrollment or service call, an automated SMS was triggered — sent from the agent's own line, timed for maximum response rate. The message was short, personal, and included a direct link to the agent's review page. No chasing. No awkward asks. The system did it automatically.

Management Dashboard

Management needed visibility, not just volume. We built a live Google Sheets dashboard tracking every agent's review velocity, response rates, and coaching flags — so managers could identify which agents were thriving and which needed support.

  • Custom landing page per agent with individual review link
  • Automated post-interaction SMS triggered from agent number
  • Management dashboard: per-agent review velocity and trends
  • Google Sheets integration for real-time reporting
  • A/B tested message timing and copy for maximum response rate

The Results

Agents now compete on reputation. Not price.

Over four years, Health for California went from 10 reviews to over 9,000 verified Google reviews — a 900x increase across their agent network. Individual agents who had been digitally invisible now had pages with hundreds of reviews, outranking competitors and closing clients before ever making a pitch.
