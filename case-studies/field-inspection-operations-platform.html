The Problem

Running a PDR operation across disconnected handoffs.

The portfolio record concerns a paintless-dent-repair and hail-restoration operator, where field crews, adjusters, office staff, and insurance systems can all touch the same claim. It identifies missing context at handoffs as the operational problem.

The recorded requirement was a system shaped around the operator's inspection, documentation, and internal-decision workflow while carrier systems remained part of the process.

The portfolio record describes an iPhone field app, a shared backend, planned claim-data integration points, and a staged operations workflow. It does not establish direct carrier integration, production coverage, or tracking through final payment.

The Intervention

An iPhone field app and shared operations record.

The portfolio record describes a custom iOS app and connected operations-platform design intended to keep field and back-office work on the same job record. The repository does not contain an accepted scope or client sign-off confirming the complete delivery boundary.

The iPhone App

The documented app design includes an active job queue, a vehicle-inspection workflow, and photo capture associated with a claim record. The intended design keeps field context connected to the backend, but production behavior is not verified.

Operations Workflow

The documented workflow includes stages for storm identification, dispatch, inspection, documentation, claim submission, scheduling, and repair sign-off, with status and assignment concepts. The repository does not verify that every stage reached production or that the design eliminated missed handoffs.

Insurance Platform Integration

The portfolio record describes planned integration points for claim data while keeping an operator-controlled record central. It does not include an integration specification or evidence of live connections to carrier platforms.

Management Dashboard

The documented management-view design includes jobs by crew, claim status, photos, documentation, and pipeline status. The repository does not verify real-time data, complete operational coverage, or a reduction in separate tools.
