The Problem

Running a national PDR operation on tools built for someone else.

Dent Experts operates at a national scale in the paintless dent repair and hail restoration business — a vertical where speed, claim accuracy, and coordination between field technicians, adjusters, and insurance carriers is everything. A single storm event can generate hundreds of vehicle inspections and repair orders in days.

The problem: every major platform they were using was built by and for the insurance companies themselves. The tools worked on the carrier's terms, moved at the carrier's pace, and gave the carrier's priorities top billing. Dent Experts needed their own system — one built around how their operation actually works.

They needed a complete digital infrastructure: an Apple application for field teams, workflow automation across the full Storm Ops lifecycle, backend system integration with insurance platforms, and a scalable foundation that could grow with their national footprint — not against it.

What We Built

A complete Apple application and custom ops platform.

We built Dent Experts a full-stack custom software solution — starting with a native Apple application for their field team and backing it with a purpose-built system that integrates directly with the insurance platforms they work with every day.

Storm Ops Flow

The core of the build is the Storm Ops Flow system — a workflow automation engine designed around the full lifecycle of a hail or dent event. From initial storm identification and vehicle triage, through inspection, documentation, claim submission, and final repair completion, every step is tracked, assigned, and moved forward without manual handoffs slowing things down.

Custom Apple Application

Field technicians needed tools that worked in the field — not a desktop portal accessed from a parking lot on a phone browser. We built a dedicated Apple application that gives field teams real-time job assignments, inspection workflows, photo capture tied directly to claim records, and instant sync back to the backend. Fast, clean, and built for how the work actually happens.

Backend System Integration

The insurance platforms Dent Experts interacts with daily aren't going anywhere — so instead of fighting them, we built deep integration into those systems. Claims data flows in and out automatically. No duplicate entry. No copying information between platforms. The custom backend acts as the command center, with the insurance carrier platforms as connected data sources rather than the system of record.

Scalable Architecture

Dent Experts operates nationally, and storm season doesn't give you a warning. The infrastructure we built scales horizontally — more users, more markets, more events — without requiring a rebuild. One system, designed to run at whatever volume the business needs it to.

  • Native Apple application for field technicians
  • Storm Ops Flow — end-to-end workflow automation
  • Backend integration with major insurance platforms
  • Real-time photo capture tied to claim records
  • Scalable architecture for national deployment
  • Custom dashboards for ops and management visibility

The Results

Competing head-to-head with the carriers' own tools.

Dent Experts now runs their Storm Ops on a platform built specifically for the way they work — not adapted from something someone else designed for a different purpose. The Apple application is live in the field. The workflow automation runs from first storm contact through final repair completion. The backend integrates directly with the insurance carriers.

National Scale

Infrastructure built to handle multiple markets and storm events simultaneously without re-engineering the stack.
