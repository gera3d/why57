The Problem

Professional websites. Limited measurement.

The portfolio record concerns solo and small-firm attorneys in Sonoma County whose websites functioned mainly as digital business cards. It describes limited visibility into whether pages or campaigns were associated with qualified inquiries.

The recorded starting point included gaps in analytics, call tracking, and channel attribution. The repository does not contain dated account exports or client records that establish the exact baseline for each firm.

The practical gap was not just positioning. It was measurement: the firms needed clearer local-search journeys and a way to connect marketing activity to calls and form submissions.
