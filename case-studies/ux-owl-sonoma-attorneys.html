The Problem

Websites that looked professional and did nothing.

Solo and small-firm attorneys in Santa Rosa, Petaluma, and Sebastopol were spending thousands of dollars a year on websites that functioned as digital business cards. They existed. They had contact forms. They looked fine. But not a single one could answer the most basic business question: is this thing actually bringing in clients?

The attorneys had no analytics setup, no call tracking, and no way to distinguish between a client who found them through Google and one referred by a colleague. Their marketing existed in a black box. When asked "what's working?", the honest answer was always the same: "I'm not sure. I think the website helps."

Meanwhile, competitors — some with noticeably weaker practices — were dominating Google Maps and organic results simply because their web presence signaled authority. These attorneys were invisible precisely where their prospective clients were looking.

What We Built

Lead-generating sites + dashboards that tell the truth.

Through UX Owl — a digital agency co-founded by Gera Yeremin — we rebuilt the attorneys' web presence from the ground up. Not just aesthetically, but architecturally. The goal wasn't a pretty website. It was a measurable lead pipeline.

Website Rebuild

We replaced the brochure sites with conversion-optimized, practice-specific landing pages targeting the exact searches their clients were making in Sonoma County. Each page was structured around the client's journey, not the attorney's ego.

LinkedIn Campaigns

Solo attorneys have significant latent networks — former colleagues, clients, referral partners — who don't know the full scope of what they do. We built LinkedIn outreach campaigns that surfaced these relationships and turned passive connections into active referrals.

Custom Reporting Dashboards

We built each attorney a simple reporting dashboard — connected to their Google Analytics, Search Console, and call tracking data — that produced a plain-English weekly summary: which channels sent visitors, which pages converted, and where they were losing people. For the first time, they had actual answers.

  • Practice-area landing pages optimized for Sonoma County local search
  • Google Business Profile optimization and review strategy
  • LinkedIn network activation campaign with scripted outreach
  • Custom reporting dashboard: calls, form fills, and channel attribution
  • Google Search Console + GA4 integration with weekly summary reports

The Results

Marketing spend that finally made sense.

On average, attorneys saw a 30–50% increase in qualified leads — not vanity traffic boosts, but actual prospects who called, submitted forms, and converted into clients.
