## Representation boundary

No retained evidence supports a federal set-aside certification, contract vehicle, government award, agency past performance, approved-vendor standing, CMMC certification, NIST SP 800-171 implementation, SPRS score, FedRAMP authorization, FISMA compliance, authority to operate, facility clearance, personnel clearance, authorized-reseller status, or a filed 57 / WHY57 fictitious business name.

Before responding to an opportunity, verify the current official record in the applicable system and apply any solicitation-specific requirements. A profile, marketplace account, or certification record does not itself establish eligibility, award, or buyer approval.
